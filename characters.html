Elder Wade Phillips

Elder Phillips has a love for the rules and regulations of the mission. His willingness to work hard, be obedient, and listen to the spirit has helped him out a lot in the past. But, his inflexibility tends to make him harder to get along with, and his shy nature tends to get in the way of getting to know people.

Elder Jimmy Herbie

Elder Herbie is first and foremost a people person. While he has some trouble staying focused, and isn't the most obedient missionary, his infectious positive attitude, love for the people, and easy-going personality still makes him an effective missionary.
